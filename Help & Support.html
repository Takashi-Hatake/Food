Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I place an order?

A: To place an order, you can visit our Menu page and select your desired items.

Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?

A: Unfortunately, once an order is placed, it cannot be modified. Please double-check your order before confirming.

Q: How can I track my order?

A: You can track your order on the My Orders page. Enter your tracking ID to get real-time updates on your delivery status.
